Senior Software Engineer - Backend

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our backend team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining scalable and reliable backend services that power our web and mobile applications.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code
- Integration of data storage solutions and third-party APIs
- Collaborate with frontend developers and other team members
- Identify bottlenecks and bugs, and devise solutions to address these issues
- Help maintain code quality, organization, and automatization

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in backend development
- Proficiency in Node.js and Express.js
- Experience with database technologies (MongoDB, PostgreSQL)
- Understanding of server-side templating languages
- Knowledge of cloud services (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Familiarity with continuous integration and deployment
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Nice to Have:
- Experience with microservices architecture
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with messaging queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- Understanding of security best practices

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Remote-friendly work environment
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
